Katsudon

Description

Katsudon is a Japanese dish consisting of rice topped with a pork or chicken cutlet and fluffy steamed eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 pork or chicken cutlets
  • flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup dashi
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp mirin
  • 1 onion
  • 1 scallion
